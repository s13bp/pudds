TAXES AND SUPPLY

TOKENOMICS

Everyone wants to moon but nobody wants to hold so what's the best solution? We believe setting targets and gradually raising sell limits at those targets is the best solution for existing holders while protecting new ones. You can't get rich if you don't hold right? No invasive taxes that take all possible gains or complex rules on when you can sell or for how much. We hit the target market caps you can sell for specified amounts not complicated.

You are allowed to sell once per day so pick wisely when you choose to sell because you might miss a higher target in that 24 hour period. All wallet to wallet transfers will be blocked so don't try to bypass sell limits it won't work for you.











MARKETING

We plan to slow build hype for several weeks leading up to presale using only proven partners to best optimize money spent. There's no reason to throw money at everything and hope it works. We are spending investors money so we need to be smart with how it's used. Post launch a majority of marketing will be done with partnerships and onboarding. If money made everything reach high market caps you'd see that result but you don't. If it doesn't make sense and we don't get a return for the money spent we won't do it. Most important phase for marketing is pre launch that's where our main focus will be.

LIQUIDITY

Initial liquidity will be locked for 3 months in order to time with the DEX launch. We don't anticipate needing a new smart contract so a full migration won't be needed just a liquidity migration from PancakeSwap to our pool. Migrations are a hassle and generally means a lot of investors left behind due to issues. We want to prevent that and make the process fast and painless for investors so we can resume trading within 24 hours no investors left behind. If the majority would rather leave it locked and do a more traditional method for migration we can leave it locked to protect liquidity.

INCENTIVES

We want to make the DEX as lucrative as possible for holders. All token holders will receive 50% of all transactions on the DEX in BNB straight to their wallets. No need to stake, no additional fees required. You hold "X" amount and you will be sent dividends. There's other avenues we are planning for revenue which will all be split same way to holders. We don't want people to sell and we need a reason for new investors to buy.

PRIVATE & PRESALE




Private sale funds will be used to hire talented coders to build out our ideas and to also market the presale in order to have buy pressure at launch. Pre-launch marketing is the most important step to have awareness and demand. Unfortunately it's a neccesary evil in this space and without it we'd be just another pump and dump with a 1 day lifespan. All private sale backers will receive a 20% bonus over presale and launch day rates. There's no vesting periods since the tokenomics are there to prevent large selloffs.





Presale ideally will be hosted through the smart contract over other platforms. Giving up 2% of supply or more to use other platforms isn't good for us or for your money. We'd be penalized for succeding and they'll capitalize by selling $1+ Million and it's your money they're taking. We've watched it happen on every succesful project and investors believe it's the team selling off but it's really the platform taking their greedy cut. We understand it's not a traditional way to do things but we're focused on the long term growth of the project instead of being short lived. We just can't allow another persons platform to be the largest holder in the project for simply hosting a presale. We won't have people connect their wallets to a dApp on the website to prevent a dusting attack. If you buy presale it will be directly through the tokens smart contract.
